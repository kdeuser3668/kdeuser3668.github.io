TOP 5 destinations
  1. Great Barrier Reef, Australia
  2. Vienna, Austria

  4. Rio de Janiero, Brazil
  5. Grace Bay Beach, Turks and Caicos

#1 Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The Great Barrier Reef, which extends for over 2,300 kilometers (1429 miles) along the northeastern coast of Australia, is home to over 9,000 known species. The biodiversity it contains is remarkable. Because of this it has some of the best snorkeling in the world. One of my favorite hobbies is snorkeling, making this my top destination.

Why the Great Barrier Reef is so special.

#2 Vienna, Austria

In 2019, for the second time running,Vienna was named the “World’s Most Liveable City”. It is a beautiful and clean city, and is widely known for it's lively culture, music, food, greenery, and architectural history. FUN FACT: One of my favorite movies growing up was The Sound of Music. I loved everything about that movie, but most of all how beautiful the scenery was. That's how I discovered Vienna in the first place :).

Why You Should visit Vienna, According to People Who Live there..

#3 Florence Italy

It has always been a personal dream of mine to one day visit Italy, so this is a top destination of mine, in my opinion, Italy is by far one of the most beautiful countries. "Florence is a place to immerse yourself in history, art and architecture. Dubbed the birthplace of the Renaissance, it’s home to works by Leonardo Da Vinci and Michelangelo at the world-famous Uffizi Gallery, plus beautiful attractions like the Ponte Vecchio and the Boboli Gardens.

The top reasons Why Everyone should visit Florence at least Once

#4 Rio de Janiero, Brazil

It is a beautiful and diverse city that is defined by its natural wonders and stunning beaches. It is also home to a complex urban life. In addition, it has one of the seven wonders of the world, Christ the Redeemer.

Why You Should visit Vienna, According to People Who Live there..

#5 Grace Bay Beach, Turks and Caicos

This is consistentley rated one of the top beaches in the world. This magnificent beach has the sublime combo of white sand and warm water. Snorkeling is also popular here, as it's underwater world consists of rays, green turtles, and all sorts of fish.
